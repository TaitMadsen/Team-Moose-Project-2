This site's purpose is to provide University of Rochester staff and personnel a form that will allow for easy creation of events and seminars that students can attend. This website links to the mobile app, "Seminar Scene", which alerts students and staff that have signed up for designated surveys with notifications that will appear on their smartphone or device.

Creation of this site was done by Team Moose.
